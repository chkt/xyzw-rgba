#xyzw-rgba

Color vectors and transforms

##Install

```sh
yarn add xyzw-rgba
```

###Use

xyzw-rgba is an extension of [xyzw](https://github.com/chkt/xyzw) providing
methods to easily work with colors and convert them from and to common color formats.
